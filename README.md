
**Context:** Pictures are an amazing invention. They are one of the only ways we can interact with time. We can’t go forward or backward but we can freeze a moment. Photos affect our perception of moments including What we choose to capture and who we share it with.

**Problem:** With smartphone there is no friction between you taking a photo, editing and share it. Along with social media this encourages people to only have picture perfect moment that are worth sharing, cause if you can have a perfect photo why not make it perfect. 

**Consuquences:** This encourages people to stage the perfect moment instead of capture the rawness of a moment. It encourages people to be taken out of the moment and start living for a photo instead taking photos of them living.

**Solution:** Photo can be a powerful to for self refection. A way to see how you spend your time and remembering important moments in life. For me photos have become a way for me to make everyday feel unique and help me live more intentionally. 

When you can clearly see how you spent your time you can reconsider if this is what you want your time to be spent doing. This is you life and you should be living a life that is intentional meaningful to you. I think cameras such a journaling can be a tool that anyone can use to reassess how they are spending their time and also be happy looking back at.
